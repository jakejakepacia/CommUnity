# CommUnity

CommUnity is a mobile application designed to make communication within communities easier, faster, and more reliable.

Whether it is a subdivision, barangay, organization, or even a simple circle of friends, CommUnity provides a centralized platform where members can stay connected, informed, and engaged.

---

## 📱 Features

### 🏘️ Community Creation
Users can create and manage their own communities such as:
- Subdivisions
- Barangays
- School organizations
- Clubs
- Friend groups

### 📢 Announcements
Community admins can post important announcements to quickly inform everyone about:
- Meetings
- Events
- Emergencies
- Community updates
- Reminders

### ⚠️ Concerns & Discussions
Members can raise concerns, start discussions, and communicate issues within the community in an organized way.

Examples:
- Security concerns
- Maintenance requests
- Lost and found
- Suggestions and feedback

### 🛒 Community Marketplace
CommUnity includes a built-in marketplace where users can:
- Buy and sell items
- Promote local services
- Support small businesses within the community

This makes community transactions more accessible and convenient.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The main goal of CommUnity is to strengthen communication and engagement within local communities by providing a reliable all-in-one platform for announcements, discussions, and local marketplace activities.
